## Query 8
**Question:** What were the main challenges identified by respondents in the FTA survey?

**Answer:**
Based on the FTA survey results, respondents identified several main challenges:

## Key Challenges Identified by Respondents:

### 1. **Limited Awareness and Training**
- Only about **one in five survey respondents** received FTA training
- Awareness levels vary significantly by region, ranging from 87% in Dire Dawa to 69% in Afar region
- Higher proportions of respondents in developing regional states (Benishangul-Gumuz, Gambela, and Afar) reported being unaware of FTA initiatives
- Limited awareness creation activities for key stakeholders at the organizational level

### 2. **Resistance to Information Disclosure**
- Resistance to provide financial information to the public, particularly regarding:
  - Procurement processes
  - Audit findings
- Only about **32% of respondents** recognize that their local government should provide procurement and audit reports to the community

### 3. **Implementation and Follow-up Issues**
- Lack of clear definitions of key stakeholders for federal institutions
- Tendency to fall back to the status quo or follow old ways of doing things
- Limited regular follow-up on findings and recommendations from previous monitoring and evaluation
- Some recommendations are too general to be useful for regional/woreda-level officials

### 4. **Persistent Inequalities**
- **Gender inequality** in access to information and participation
- Unequal access to budgetary information between female and rural residents
- Social, cultural, and institutional factors hinder equal participation

### 5. **Service Quality Concerns**
- Two out of three respondents remain **dissatisfied with the quality of local services**
- High complaints specifically regarding **water and health services**

### 6. **Coordination and Accountability**
- Need to strengthen accountability mechanisms
- Need to improve coordination with other citizen engagement programs
- Inter-ethnic and inter-regional conflicts may explain low performance in certain regions

### 7. **Attribution Problem**
- Difficulty identifying the exact impact of FTA since many factors affect transparency and accountability of local government
- Cannot establish counterfactual (what would have happened without FTA)

## Query 9
**Question:** How was the data collected for the 2022 performance survey?

**Answer:**
Based on the document search, the data for the 2022 performance survey was collected using the following methodology:

**Sampling Design:**
The survey followed the sampling frame of baseline surveys from 2009 and 2016. The sample design was a **multi-stage random cluster sample** with the following stages:

1. **First stage:** Woreda selection
2. **Second stage:** Kebele selection (Kebeles are the primary sampling units)
3. **Third stage:** Household selection from each kebele

**Sampling Process:**
- Woredas were selected from each region/city administration
- Kebeles were selected from each woreda
- Households were selected from each kebele

**Coverage:**
The study covered:
- 39 federal institutions (including federal-level universities)
- 10 regions
- 2 city administrations
